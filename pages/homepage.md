## Solver Tools

Our solver tools represent some of the most advanced puzzle-solving assistance available online. The Wordle Solver supports all word lengths from 4 to 12 letters, making it useful not just for standard Wordle but for any word-guessing puzzle you might encounter. The solver uses entropy-based analysis to suggest the most informative guesses.

The Quordle Solver helps you crack all four words simultaneously, taking into account the letters you have already guessed and their positions across all four boards. This solver is particularly valuable for players who want to maintain high scores in this challenging variant.

For Betweenle players, our solver accepts your upper and lower bounds along with distance percentages to suggest the optimal next guess. This tool is essential for mastering the unique sorted-word mechanics that make Betweenle so distinctive.

Our specialized solvers extend to games like Nerdle for math equations, Waffle for grid puzzles, Colordle for color matching, and many more. Each solver is tailored to the specific mechanics of its game, ensuring relevant and helpful suggestions.
