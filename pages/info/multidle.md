## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is Multidle?
Multidle is a Wordle variant that lets you play puzzles with word lengths ranging from 4 to 12 letters. It offers varying difficulty levels in one convenient game, from quick 4-letter challenges to extended 12-letter vocabulary tests.

### How many guesses do I get in Multidle?
Like standard Wordle, Multidle gives you six attempts to guess the hidden word regardless of word length. The familiar green, yellow, and gray feedback system helps you narrow down the answer with each guess.

### Is Multidle free to play?
Yes, Multidle on WordSolverX is completely free to play with no registration required. You can enjoy daily puzzles across all supported word lengths without any cost.

### Can I use the Wordle Solver for Multidle puzzles?
Yes, our Wordle Solver supports all word lengths from 4 to 12 letters, making it perfectly suited for helping you solve Multidle puzzles at any difficulty level.
