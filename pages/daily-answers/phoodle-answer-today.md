## Understanding Phoodle

Phoodle is a daily word puzzle game with a food theme. Each day, players must guess a five-letter word related to food — this could be an ingredient, a cooking technique, a dish, a kitchen tool, or any other food-related term. The game uses the same color-coded feedback system as Wordle: green means the letter is correct and in the right position, yellow means the letter is in the word but in the wrong spot, and gray means the letter isn't in the word at all. The Phoodle daily puzzle resets every 24 hours, giving food lovers a fresh challenge each day.

Learning how to play Phoodle is simple if you've played Wordle before, but the food theme adds an extra layer of challenge. You need to think about culinary vocabulary — from common ingredients like "basil" and "olive" to cooking methods like "braise" and "saute." Phoodle rules follow the standard Wordle format: you have six attempts to guess the five-letter food word, and each guess provides feedback to help you narrow it down. The key is thinking within the food domain while using the same elimination strategies that work in Wordle.
