## Understanding Worgle

Worgle is a daily word puzzle game that challenges players to guess a hidden word within a limited number of attempts. Similar to Wordle but with its own unique mechanics and twists, Worgle provides color-coded feedback after each guess to help players narrow down the correct answer. Green indicates a letter in the correct position, yellow means a letter is in the word but in the wrong spot, and gray means the letter is not in the word at all. The Worgle daily puzzle resets every 24 hours, giving players a fresh word to discover each day.

Learning how to play Worgle is straightforward if you're familiar with word guessing games. You enter a valid word as your guess, and the game provides feedback on each letter. Worgle rules give you a set number of attempts to find the hidden word, and the challenge is to use the feedback efficiently to arrive at the correct answer within those attempts. The game appeals to word game enthusiasts who enjoy a daily vocabulary challenge and the satisfaction of deducing the correct word through logic and linguistic knowledge.
