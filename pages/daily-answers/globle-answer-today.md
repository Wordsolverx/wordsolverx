## Understanding Globle

Globle is a daily geography game where players must guess a mystery country on an interactive 3D globe. After each guess, the game colors the guessed country on a gradient from pale (far away) to dark red (very close), indicating how geographically proximate your guess is to the target country. The Globle daily puzzle resets every 24 hours, giving players a new country to discover each day. It's an engaging way to test your knowledge of world geography and learn about countries you might not be familiar with.

Learning how to play Globle is intuitive but mastering it requires strong geographic knowledge and strategic thinking. You start by guessing any country in the world, and the globe provides color-coded feedback showing how close your guess is. Globle rules allow unlimited guesses, and there's no penalty for incorrect attempts — the goal is simply to find the mystery country as quickly as possible. The color gradient ranges from light yellow (very far) to deep red (very close), making it easy to understand your proximity at a glance.
