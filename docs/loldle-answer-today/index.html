Understanding LoLdle

LoLdle is a daily puzzle game inspired by Wordle but designed specifically for League of Legends players. Each day, players must guess a mystery champion from the League of Legends roster by making guesses and receiving feedback on various champion attributes. These attributes typically include the champion's gender, position, species, resource, range type, release year, and region. The LoLdle daily puzzle offers multiple modes, including Classic, Quote, Ability, Emoji, and Splash Art, each providing a different way to identify the champion.

Learning how to play LoLdle requires a good understanding of League of Legends' extensive champion roster. You guess champions and receive feedback showing which attributes match and which don't, helping you narrow down the possibilities. LoLdle rules vary by mode — in Classic mode, you guess based on attributes, while other modes test your knowledge of champion quotes, abilities, emoji representations, and splash art. The more you know about every champion in the game, the better you'll perform across all modes.
