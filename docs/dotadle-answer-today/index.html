Understanding Dotadle

Dotadle is a daily guessing game designed for fans of Dota 2. Each day, players must identify a mystery Dota 2 hero by guessing heroes and receiving feedback on how closely their guess matches the target. The feedback typically includes attributes such as the hero's primary attribute (Strength, Agility, or Intelligence), attack type (melee or ranged), roles, and other characteristics. The Dotadle daily puzzle resets every 24 hours, providing a fresh hero to guess each day.

Learning how to play Dotadle is simple if you're familiar with Dota 2's extensive roster of heroes. You start by guessing any hero, and the game provides feedback comparing your guess to the target hero across multiple categories. Dotadle rules reward knowledge of hero attributes and roles, so the more you know about Dota 2's diverse hero pool, the better you'll perform. The game is a fun way to test your Dota 2 knowledge and learn more about heroes you may not play often.
