Understanding Betweenle

Betweenle is a daily word puzzle that puts your alphabetical knowledge to the test. Each day, you're given two words and must figure out the mystery word that falls between them in alphabetical order. The game provides feedback after each guess, telling you whether your guess is alphabetically before or after the target word, helping you narrow down the possibilities. The Betweenle daily puzzle resets every 24 hours, giving players a fresh challenge each day.

Learning how to play Betweenle is simple but mastering it takes practice. You start by reviewing the two boundary words provided at the top of the puzzle. Your job is to think of a word that comes alphabetically between them. After each guess, the game adjusts the boundaries to help you zero in on the correct answer. Betweenle rules are straightforward: guess a valid English word, and the boundaries will update accordingly until you find the exact word. The fewer guesses you use, the better your score.
