Understanding Canuckle

Canuckle is a Canadian-themed Wordle game that challenges players to guess a five-letter word related to Canada. From famous Canadian cities and landmarks to iconic foods, slang, and cultural references, every Canuckle daily puzzle celebrates all things Canadian. The game uses the same color-coded feedback system as Wordle — green means the letter is correct and in the right position, yellow means the letter is in the word but in the wrong spot, and red (instead of Wordle's gray) means the letter isn't in the word at all.

Learning how to play Canuckle is easy if you're familiar with Wordle, but the Canadian twist adds an extra layer of difficulty. You need to think about Canadian geography, culture, history, and everyday vocabulary to come up with the right guess. Canuckle rules follow the standard Wordle format: you have six attempts to guess the five-letter word, and each guess provides feedback to help you narrow it down.
