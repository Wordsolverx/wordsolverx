Understanding Spotle

Spotle is a daily guessing game designed for music lovers. Each day, players must identify a mystery artist by guessing musicians and receiving feedback on various attributes such as the artist's genre, debut decade, nationality, gender, and whether they're part of a group or solo. The Spotle daily puzzle resets every 24 hours, providing a fresh artist to identify each day. It's a fun way to test your music knowledge and discover artists you might not be familiar with.

Learning how to play Spotle is simple if you enjoy music trivia. You start by guessing any artist, and the game provides feedback comparing your guess to the target across multiple categories. Spotle rules reward broad music knowledge — the more artists you know across different genres, decades, and countries, the better you'll perform. The game draws from a wide pool of artists, from global superstars to indie favorites, making each day's puzzle a unique challenge.
